The Story

Meet Josiah

"Turn your memories into magnets!"

Hi, I'm Josiah — the kid behind JMagnet Co. What started as a simple idea to turn photos into something people could keep forever has grown into a real business selling custom magnets, buttons, keychains, and bottle openers across Texas.

I started with sheet magnets, then expanded into round products after seeing what people actually wanted. Now I'm out at local vendor markets, taking custom orders, and working with businesses on bulk runs — all while running everything myself.

Every order is made with care. Kid-owned, Texas-made, and built to last.
